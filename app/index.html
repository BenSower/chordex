The chord diagram

Chord diagrams show relationships among a group of entities. In this example visualizing the PubDb, a database containing the paper-releases of the mmi-faculty of the lmu. Use the filters on the right to only display people who (co-)wrote a certain amount of publications and/or worked with at least a specified number of people in a certain year. Publications with only a single author will be ignored.
